Our Vision

Almost all the problems created by COVID-19 have been solved through technology. Respectively, we decided to solve the issue of insecurity of a doctor's visit during this period. The idea is for a website that serves patients online. The novelty of the idea is that it offers medical help without exposing the patient to the virus. The home page of the site contains COVID-19 updates and information about our doctors. The website is staffed by doctors of all specialties who do not need surgery or examination. The patient is required to make an account in order to make an appointment with the doctor of his choice. The patient has the opportunity to see the free days and hours of the doctor and to choose which one he prefers through the calendar function. He will then receive an update by mail which will include the date of the appointment, the doctor's details and the link he will enter to meet the doctor. We believe that our website will open a new chapter in medicine and that it will significantly help the current situation.
